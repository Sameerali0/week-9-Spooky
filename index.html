Ghost Catcher

How to Play

  • Tap on the ghosts as they appear to catch them.
  • You have 30 seconds to catch as many as you can.
  • Each ghost gives you 1 point.
  • You can turn music on/off from the top-right corner.
  • Missed ghosts disappear automatically after a short time.
